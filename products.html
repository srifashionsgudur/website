Product FAQs

Before you enquire.

How can I check product prices?

Use the WhatsApp enquiry button on a product card. The product name is added automatically so our boutique team can share price and availability.

Can I reserve an outfit on WhatsApp?

Yes. Send the product name on WhatsApp and ask about reservation, appointment slots and store pickup.

Do you provide custom stitching?

Yes. Sri Fashions Boutique provides custom measurements, blouse design support and outfit finishing consultation.

Can I visit the store before buying?

Yes. You can visit Sri Fashions Boutique near Gudur Junction Railway, Gudur. Opening hours are placeholders until launch.

Are all products ready-made?

Some styles are ready to enquire, while custom fits and handwork pieces may require consultation and stitching time.
